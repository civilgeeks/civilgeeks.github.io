Online Solvers for Mechanics of Solid Verification

Hasi Syed October 16, 2019

A solver is a piece of mathematical software that 'solves' a mathematical problem. Solvers are helpful in verification of manual calculations. A list of free to use solvers is provided in this post.

Beam Solvers:

Beam solvers solve for different parameters of beams.

https://skyciv.com/free-beam-calculator/

http://beamguru.com/online/beam-calculator/

 Sectional Property Solvers:

https://skyciv.com/free-moment-of-inertia-calculator/

https://www.amesweb.info/SectionalPropertiesTabs/SectionalPropertiesCbeam.aspx

https://calcresource.com/cross-section-channel.html
