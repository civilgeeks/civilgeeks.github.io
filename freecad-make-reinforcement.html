FreeCAD Make Reinforcement

Hasi Syed June 24, 2019
These steps are to be performed in BIM workbench;

1. Create BIM structure and select its face.
2. Open Reinforcement tool, select reinforcement type.
    Enter its properties.
3. Pick the selected face and press apply, then ok.
    Change the transperency of BIM Object to `View > Transperency` to 35%
    to see the reinforcement inside.
